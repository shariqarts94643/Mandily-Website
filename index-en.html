What is Mandily?

Mandily is a biodegradable towel tablet product that is as compact as ¾ inch in diameter of a recyclable package, inside which the 10” x 10.5” natural, additive-free viscose cloth is compressed and contained. The towel is kept dry inside the tiny package, until the Mandily user activates the system with a single push of a finger in the center of the Mandily tablet. The dry towel then turns into a wet disposable towelette that is ready to use after 4 seconds.
